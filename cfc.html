Center for Creativity at the Rialto

Westfield Arts Collective

For this project, I worked with a local art collective to create a website for their new community space. I began by meeting with the organization to understand their mission and needs. As a new organization, the Westfield Arts Collective was still in the process of developing their plan for the space and wanted a high degree of flexibility for the website. Their primary needs for the website were to inform community members and increase awareness, collect donations, and gather volunteer information.

Goal

Raise awareness of a new non-profit organization in Westfield by creating a public website

Timeline

October 2021

Skills

  • Web design
  • Consulting

Tools

  • Wix

Process

After consulting with the organization, we decided that Wix would be the best platform for building the website. I began this process by outlining the pages and information architecture of the website. Once I established a structure that met the user's needs, I began to flesh out the content of the website. I chose a color scheme that matched the colors of the organization's logo and picked a minimalist sans-serif font for accessibility. I then added textual content and images using the organization's business plans and branding documents as a model.

Once these basic elements of the site were created, I focused on increasing the functionality of the site. I created a form to capture data from users who were interested in volunteering and integrated a PayPal donation form for those who wished to donate. I also optimized the website for search engines and accessibility, ensuring there was alternative text and sufficient color contrast on every page.
